How can I mine bitcoin ?

Before you start mining Bitcoin, you must have a BitCoin wallet.
The BitCoin network compensates Bitcoin miners for their effort by releasing
bitcoin to those who contribute the needed computational power.
You must also have a mining hardware (good graphic card). Then you can download NiceHash
You can also look at the NiceHash website for more informations.
